Introducing myself to the world

My name is nia, I'm 22 years old, I was born and live in Brazil.
I'm known on the internet as simply, kreed.
I tend to be a gamer modder, my skills involve creating characters and rigging models. I can rig pretty much everything: humans, animals, monsters you name it!

The beginning

Since I was a child, games have always been my passion, I spent hours playing and the fun never ended.

I became curious about how games were made, I dove headfirst into engines like Unity, Unreal and Source, using Valve Hammer Editor.
Over time I tried to understand how textures and materials worked, so tried to understand Photoshop and ended up with Blender.
I never saw myself being a game developer but I always knew I had a lot of potential to do something useful, that's when I joined the modding community.

When I started

The first game I had the pleasure of modifying was Counter-Strike 1.6, creating small changes to the maps when playing with friends, then we all started playing Left 4 Dead 2 and I thought, "why not?" So I learned how to port custom models to this game.
A while later I realized that was making mods for all the games I played, it was like that with my favorites:
Resident Evil 4 (2005), Resident Evil 5, Resident Evil 2,3,4 Remake, 7 Days to Die and others.

My beloved games

It's a fact, I grew up playing Capcom and Valve games. Apart from L4D2 which I already mentioned, I'm also a big fan of Portal, Half-Life and Counter-Strike.
Survival horror I simply have all Resident Evil purchased on Steam, this is just the beginning to describe how much I love the RE franchise.

Community scene

At the beginning of 2022 I decided to join the REModding Community, a forum mainly for Resident Evil Games.
I immediately received the “modder” tag for sharing my Resident Evil 4 (2005) mods and I didn't expect people to find the modifications I made to the game so surprising.

I loved the idea that people liked what I did, I received a lot of compliments and that encouraged me even more to learn about modding.

I started making tutorials on how to modify RE4 and stayed in that environment for a while, after all I ended up receiving a lot of mod commissions and I paid attention to each one of them, it was becoming almost a job.

So Resident Evil 4 Remake was announced, I was in shock, my favorite game would be remade.
I started learning how to make mods for RE Engine, thus making many ports for Resident Evil 2 and 3 Remake.
At the same time as I updated my projects for RE4 (2005) I continued accepting commissions for RE2 Remake.

Months passed and a friend who is a big fan of Resident Evil 5 asked for my help in porting the models he had created. Until then, I had never modified this game.
A few days later I was releasing my first mod for RE5.
To this day I still receive requests for this game, as well I still have some projects in progress.

What's to come

As you saw, I'm a person who runs after their goals and I don't know the word "impossible" many times I proved people wrong when they said something was impossible. I am a person who learns easily and quickly, someone willing to learn and grow more every day.
I am a person who stays active in the community in which I was raised, I help people with difficulties in things that I had in the past and teach them to evolve just like I did.

I'm searching for a place where I can use my skills, in the meantime wait for new mods and updates for my projects.
