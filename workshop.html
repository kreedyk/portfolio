    CS:GO Major Rio

    On October 31, 2022, Valve would hold the Major Championship in my country, the IEM Rio Major 2022.
    As a fan of the scene and for the first time a major in my country, I decided to make a CS:GO map in honor of it, with many Brazilian references.
